Animal Kingdom

Tiger

  • Largest cat species
  • Distinctive orange coat with black stripes
  • Solitary and territorial
  • Primarily found in Asia

Komodo Dragon

  • World's largest living lizard
  • Native to Indonesian islands
  • Venomous bite
  • Carnivorous diet

Hawk

  • Sharp talons and beak
  • Excellent vision
  • Diurnal birds of prey
  • Found worldwide

Eagle

  • Large, powerful birds of prey
  • Symbol of strength and freedom
  • Hooked beak and strong talons
  • Found on every continent except Antarctica

Vulture

  • Scavengers feeding on carrion
  • Bald head to stay clean while feeding
  • Important for ecosystem health
  • Found worldwide
